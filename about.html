ABOUT ME

Hello there! Welcome to my corner of the internet, where technology meets humor, and coding errors makes your patience ran out.

I’m Rizalyn Oyangorin, 19, residing in Iligan City, an IT enthusiast who was born pretty and firmly believes that the only bugs worth having are in video games (and even those can be a pain!).

MY JOURNEY

My fascination with tech started when I first tried to fix a family computer and ended up deleting half of the desktop icons. Since then, I’ve been on a relentless mission to conquer the digital realm — one line of code, server crash, or overly complicated tech manual at a time.

WHAT I DO

  • Building apps and websites that work better than my Brain during discussions.
  • Troubleshooting issues that turn others into IT support memes.
  • Exploring new tech trends because who doesn’t love hours of watching tutorials?.

MY PHILOSOPHY

In the ever-evolving world of IT, I live by two golden rules:

  • Restart it first.
  • If that doesn’t work, pretend you’re debugging while furiously Googling the solution.

WHY I LOVE IT

Technology is more than a career for me; it’s a way to make life simpler, smarter, and a little more fun and occasionally, an excuse to explain to people why their printer isn’t working.

WHEN I'M NOT GEEKING OUT...

  • Binge-watching sci-fi shows and imagining a world where my code powers the spaceships.
  • Arguing with AI assistants because, let’s face it, they’re only as smart as we make them.
  • Explaining to friends and family why "No, I can’t hack into your ex’s social media account."
